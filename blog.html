Monday

Monday is the first day of the week. It is the day after Sunday. This Monday, I dragged myself out of bed at 8:00 am for school. I felt nervous for finals week, but also excited for the feeling of accomplishment at the end. Many people hate mondays, but I don't think they should. We should all appreciate the new week and have a positive attitude!

Tuesday

Tuesdays are green to me. Let me explain. I used to always have art class on Tuesdays while in elementary school. After class ended, I would skip down to the library for art class with my light green sketchbook in hand. Ever sense, I have always viewed Tuesday as a green day. I spent this Tuesday in a hurry due to all A-H periods being packed into a day!

Wednesday

Time to start looking forward to the weekend! I mean, it's halfway already! Wednesday is a fun day because I no longer think about what day it is. Monday and Tuesday, I think about how early in the week it is. Thursday and Friday, I'm close to the end! Wednesday is the most... average day of all days. But doesn't that in itself make it unique?

Thursday

A comfortable day to nap after school! As soon as I hit my bed at 3:30, I'm ready to lose consciousness. Then, I lounge about until dinner time. Then... I panic at the thought of starting my assignments at 7:00pm! But who can blame me? It's Thursday, of course I'm tired. This thought process causes me to procrastinate for at least another two hours... oh, well.

Friday

Excitement for the weekend!!! Friday is a day of false hopes and dreams. "I'm going to lock in this weekened for sure." Falser words have never been spoken. This Friday, I need to study for the SAT... which I haven't started studying for... which is in a few days...
